# Code of Conduct

VIGIL is an open governance and evidence repository. Contributors are expected to participate respectfully and handle claims about platforms, individuals, organisations, and public incidents with care.

## Expected behaviour

* Be respectful, constructive, and evidence-oriented.
* Distinguish evidence, interpretation, uncertainty, and opinion.
* Treat public-user reports and social-media claims as provisional unless corroborated.
* Preserve privacy and minimise unnecessary personal information.
* Raise evidence, schema, security, or record-boundary concerns through review rather than personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behaviour

* Harassment, threats, intimidation, or abusive language.
* Doxxing or publishing private personal data.
* Posting secrets, credentials, private keys, tokens, private chat exports, or private account data.
* Fabricating evidence, URLs, screenshots, dates, publishers, or source claims.
* Using VIGIL records to target individuals or make unsupported claims of wrongdoing.
* Publishing sensitive vulnerability or exploit details where disclosure would enable abuse.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, redact, reject, or remove contributions that violate this code, create security risk, expose private information, or materially weaken evidence integrity. Repeated or severe violations may result in loss of contribution privileges.
