## 1. Purpose

VIGIL is a public evidence-to-repair governance ledger for documenting AI-system observations, failure modes, proposals, patch notes, taxonomy classifications, source records, and governance-relevant repair pathways.

VIGIL is maintained as part of the wider Caelestis Architecture Model governance ecosystem. It is intended to support public-interest research, AI governance analysis, policy development, safety review, civil-society monitoring, and accountable documentation of emergent AI-system failure patterns.
